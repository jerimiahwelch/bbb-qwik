The KitchenAid Artisan Tilt-Head Stand Mixer was built to last, features 10 speeds to gently knead, thoroughly mix & whip ingredients for a variety of recipes, and the capacity to make 9 dozen cookies when using the flat beater, 28g dough each.
  • Durable, built to last metal construction
  • 59 Touchpoints around bowl for great mixing
  • 10 Speeds for Nearly any Task or Recipe
  • 5 Quart Stainless Steel Bowl with comfortable handle to mix up to 9 dozen cookies* in a single batch. *Using the flat beater; 28g dough each
  • Easily add ingredients with the tilt-head design
  • Includes:
    • Flat beater
    • Dough hook
    • Wire whip
    • Pouring shield
  • 325-watt motor
  • Metal/plastic
  • Wipe clean exterior; dishwasher safe parts/attachments
  • 5 qt. polished stainless steel bowl
  • Measures 8.75" L x 14.3" W x 13.3" H
  • Cord measures 48"" L
  • Weighs 26 lb.
  • UL listed
  • 1-year limited manufacturer's warranty
  • 10+ attachments to make more with your mixer. *Sold separately
  • Made in USA
